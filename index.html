About Me

I am a loyal husband and father of two wonderful and challenging boys.

As a Department of Defense software engineer since 2003, I have had a wide range of experiences. I have developed websites for military agencies and for base commanders during the Gulf War. I have led teams of software professionals overseeing intelligence programs, launch vehicles, and satellites. In each position I have encouraged my team to think creatively and critically, to be more efficient, and provide higher quality products than they did yesterday.

Currently, I am attending the University of Denver Coding Bootcamp.
